About Letflix

Letflix is one of the world's leading entertainment services with 500 million paid memberships in over 200 countries enjoying TV series, documentaries, and feature films across a wide variety of genres and languages. Members can watch as much as they want, anytime, anywhere, on any internet-connected screen. Members can play, pause and resume watching, all without commercials or commitments.

CEO of Letflix

Abeluzius Markman

Letflix was first founded in August of 1990 by Abeluzius Markman. The company began out in New Zealand and has grown to become one of the world's leading internet entertainment platforms. When it first opened, Letflix was a website movie rental service. Users ordered movies on the Let flix website, and received DVDs in the post. When they were finished with them, they would simply post them back to Letflix in the envelopes provided. Abel started to think that he want to make an application that entertain people that can access anytime, anywhere, on any internet-connected screen. Members can play, pause and resume watching, all without commercials or commitments. After that letflix was born.
